#
# Example search criteria for social and behavioral research
# Focus: Youth behavior and intervention studies

BLOCK 1: Population/Demographics
adolescent*, teenager*, youth*, "young adult*", student*
"school age*", juvenile*, "age* 12-18", "age* 13-19"
child*, "young people", "school children"

BLOCK 2: Behavior/Phenomena
behavior*, behaviour*, attitude*, perception*, "social behavior*"
"risk behavior*", "health behavior*", "academic performance"
"peer influence", "social interaction*", "psychological well-being"

BLOCK 3: Research Methods
survey*, interview*, questionnaire*, "mixed method*", "qualitative"
"quantitative", "longitudinal study", "cross-sectional"
"experimental design", "observational study", "case study"

BLOCK 4: Interventions/Outcomes
intervention*, program*, "prevention program*", education*
"behavior change", "skill development", "social skill*"
effectiveness*, outcome*, result*, improvement*, "program evaluation"
